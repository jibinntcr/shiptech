SHIP TECHNOLOGY LIBRARY CUSAT

Ship Technology Library (DST Library) is one of the best and largest libraries in the university that caters the information needs of students, teachers and research scholars belonging to the Naval Architecture discipline. The library caters the information needs of a community of more than 250 users including faculty members, research scholars B.Tech and M.Tech students. The library has more than 10000 books, project reports/dissertations and 8 foreign journals.

Digital Library

60 CUSAT Digital library offers access to past question papers, news items, syllabuses and other e-contents. Past B.Tech Naval Architecture Question papers are available in the digital library.

Opening Hours

The Library remains open on all working days of the Department. It remains closed on even Saturdays, Sundays and other Holidays. The Library is open from 9AM to 4PM
